# Auto Assembler

## Specifications
*   **Dimensions:** 3x3x3.
*   **Structure:**
    *   **Controller:** Center of front face.
    *   **Body:** `factorycore:machine_casing`.
*   **Power:**
    *   Capacity: 1,000,000 FE.
    *   Usage: Based on recipe complexity.
*   **Operation:**
    *   Internal inventory maps to 3x3 crafting grid.
    *   "Lock Recipe" button (or ghost items).
    *   Automatically pulls items from internal buffer to craft result.

## Automation
*   Compatible with standard pipes/hoppers.
